The Solar System

The home system of humans, this system rests in one of the many arms of the Milky Way galaxy, and may seem somewhat familiar to you... The star here is simply named Sun, a showing of what little the inhabitants understood about the vastness of space during their time - or, perhaps, a show of self-centeredness, depending on who you ask.

A good amount of gummisaurs call places in this system home as well, enjoying the benefits of a relatively safe, although somewhat secretive, part of space.
